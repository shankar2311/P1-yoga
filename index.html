About Yoga

Yoga is a fundamental concept in Indian philosophy, psychology and religion and was in older Indian tradition a designation for a number of different physical and contemplative techniques

Shala is a word in Sanskrit meaning home. A yoga shala, is a home where yoga is shared and experienced. Traditionally yoga is a spiritual practice to attain a higher state of consciousness or liberation.

Our Vision

We believe in helping everyone know their true power and strength when it comes to mind and body. There is a whole lot of things other than just learning how to teach yoga, it is more about sharing your knowledge with someone and helping them realize how much better life could be.
