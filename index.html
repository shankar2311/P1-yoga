About Yoga

Yoga is a fundamental concept in Indian philosophy, psychology and religion and was in older Indian tradition a designation for a number of different physical and contemplative techniques. Shala is a word in Sanskrit meaning home. A yoga shala, is a home where yoga is shared and experienced. Traditionally yoga is a spiritual practice to attain a higher state of consciousness or liberation.A Hindu spiritual and ascetic discipline, a part of which, including breath control, simple meditation, and the adoption of specific bodily postures, is widely practiced for health and relaxation.
